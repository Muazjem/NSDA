Chocolate Cake

This is a simple and delicious Chocolate cake recipe you can make at home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour

  • 1 cup sugar

  • 1 cup milk

  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder

  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C

  2. Mix the flour,sugar and cocoa in a bowl

  3. Add eggs and milk, then stir well

  4. Pour into a baking tray

  5. Bake for 30 mins

Enjoy!

Serve warm with a scoop of vanila icecream
